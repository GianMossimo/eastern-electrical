About Us

Eastern Electrical Solutions (previously ED Services) is a rapidly growing, full-service electrical company founded in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. Licensed in both North Carolina and Virginia, Eastern Electrical Solutions prides itself on exceptional customer service, a strong business network, and providing the highest quality electrical services at affordable prices. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and building long-lasting relationships with our clients. While we specialize in custom lake homes, if you need electrical repairs, installations, or maintenance, we have the skills and expertise to get the job done right the first time. Generac




With a focus on providing reliable backup power solutions, Generac stands out as a leading manufacturer in the industry. From ensuring homes are protected during outages to supporting the uninterrupted operation of businesses and industrial facilities, Generac's diverse range of products caters to various needs with efficiency and effectiveness.


Generator Purchase and Hookup


Our selection includes highly dependable, long-lasting, efficient, and eco-conscious generators and power systems. Since power outages can occur unexpectedly, the best defense is preparedness. Generators offer a proactive solution to safeguard against emergency disruptions.


Generator Servicing - Coming Soon!


Regardless of whether it's your residence, workplace, commercial establishment, or industrial facility relying on a Generac backup generator, regular annual servicing is essential. This includes tasks such as oil and filter changes, fuel and voltage checks, as well as overall maintenance to ensure optimal performance. Given the recurring ice storms, strong winds, and heavy rain experienced in North Carolina and Virginia each year, power outages lasting hours or even days are not uncommon. Failing to maintain your backup generator adequately could leave you without crucial amenities like heating, cooling, refrigeration, and lighting during critical times.
